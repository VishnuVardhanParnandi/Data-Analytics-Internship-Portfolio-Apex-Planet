## 📊 Final Capstone Project

### 🔹 Project: Sales Optimization & Customer Segmentation

**Objective:**
To analyze sales data, identify revenue opportunities, and validate insights using statistical methods.

### 🔍 Key Insights

* 📍 West Region generates highest revenue
* 💻 Technology category has highest profit margins
* 📈 Q1 shows strong sales spike
* 👥 Customer segmentation reveals high-value & at-risk customers

### 🧪 Statistical Validation

* **Test:** Independent T-Test
* **P-Value:** 0.109 (> 0.05)
* **Conclusion:** No significant difference between Consumer & Corporate segments

➡️ **Business Decision:** Use a unified marketing strategy

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* ✅ Unified Marketing Campaign
* 🎯 Focus on High-Revenue Regions
* 🔄 Win-Back Campaigns for Lost Customers
* 🏆 Loyalty Programs for Best Customers
